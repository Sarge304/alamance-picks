About Alamance Picks

Alamance Picks is a free directory of locally-owned businesses in Alamance County that Sean has personally visited and endorsed. Each business gets a time-limited discount code — a way for Sean to give back to the community and drive foot traffic to the shops that make our county special.

This app is non-partisan and focused purely on supporting local business. No reviews, no negativity — just community.

For Business Owners

Want to be featured? Reach out! Sean is actively visiting businesses across Burlington, Mebane, Graham, Gibsonville, and Elon. There's no cost — just a genuine endorsement and a short discount code to help bring in new customers.
